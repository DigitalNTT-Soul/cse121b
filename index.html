Welcome to the Dice Expression Calculator

This calculator will take any dice expression similar to those found in common TTRPG games like Dungeons & Dragons and provide both the brief, summarized result of the expression and the detailed result showing the result of each individual die.

In the box below, please enter your dice expression. You can include any number of die-rolls, and use mathematical order of operations to make a formula of them. For example,
1d20 ^ (2d12 * 3d10) / 4d8 + 5d6 - 6d4

. If you are familiar with Javascript's Math library, you can use those commands as well, such as
Math.floor(1d100/1d20)
.
